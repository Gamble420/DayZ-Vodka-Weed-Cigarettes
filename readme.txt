Readme Instructions To Add Vodka Bottle, Cannabis Seeds & Cigarettes To DayZ

By applying these edits to your types.xml file your players will be able to find Vodka Bottles (only filled with water I'm afraid), packs
of Cannabis Seeds (which they can grow) and three different types of cigarette packets (which you can't smoke).
